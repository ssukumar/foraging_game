Instructions

Thank you for participating in our study!

Your goal is to collect as many apples as you can from virtual apple trees.

At the start of the game, the screen will look like this:

Instructions

To collect apples from the tree, you hit the spacebar. Each time you hit the spacebar, a green rectangle will grow within the bar on the right side of the screen.

                       

Instructions

When the green rectangle reaches the top, you receive your apples.

                       

Instructions

A blue number will then appear on the left side of the screen indicating the number of apples you received from the tree on that round. You can also see the size of your harvest by the number of apples appearing on the ground below the tree.

A running score will be updated on the top left indicating your total number of harvested apples. You want to make this number as large as possible.

                       

Instructions

You always have two options.

One option is to continue harvesting apples from the current tree. You do this by hitting the spacebar.

The second option is to move to another tree. You do this by hitting the Enter key.

However, after hitting the Enter key, you will experience a delay before the next tree appears.

                       

Instructions

You will have two rounds of 15 minutes to collect as many apples as possible. A countdown timer will indicate how much time you have left for the current round.

                       

Instructions

Remember, you always have two options:

  • Spacebar: Continue harvesting from the current tree.
  • Enter key: Move to a new tree.
                       

Instructions

You will get a break between Rounds 1 and 2.

You will receive a completion code at the end of Round 2 which will be used to authorize payment.

The game will be terminated if you leave the game screen or fail to play the game (that is, have an extended pause between hitting the Spacebar or Enter key).

You will not be paid if the game is terminated for these reasons.

Remember: The goal is to collect as many apples as possible.
